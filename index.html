Monkey D. Luffy the main character and hero of the popular Japanese anime "ONE PIECE". He is the captain of the famous Straw Hat pirates, and he has the power of the Mika-Mika devil fruit granting him the power to manipulate his body like rubber.


Roronoa Zoro is the main side character and first mate of the Straw Hats. Zoro is a swordsman who has mastered the 3 Japanese sword style, The One Sword Style(itto-ryu), The Two Sword Style(Nitoryu) and The Three Sword Style(Santoryu).
